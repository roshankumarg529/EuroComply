## 🎯 What is EuroComply?

EuroComply is an **agentic AI platform** that automatically analyses your company's compliance posture against major EU regulations. Instead of spending weeks with consultants, you get a **prioritised action plan with article-level citations** in under 5 minutes.

> *"EU compliance consulting costs €500K+ per engagement. EuroComply automates the analysis layer."*

## 🏛️ Regulations Covered

| Regulation | Full Name | Who It Applies To | Max Penalty |
|:---:|---|---|:---:|
| **GDPR** | General Data Protection Regulation | Any company processing EU personal data | €20M / 4% turnover |
| **EU AI Act** | Artificial Intelligence Act | Companies deploying or developing AI in the EU | €35M / 7% turnover |
| **NIS2** | Network & Information Security Directive 2 | Essential & important entities (energy, health, digital…) | €10M / 2% turnover |
| **DORA** | Digital Operational Resilience Act | Financial entities & ICT service providers | €10M / 2% turnover |
| **CSRD** | Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive | Companies with 250+ employees in the EU | Public disclosure |

## 🚀 How to Use EuroComply

### Step 1 — Sign In

Create a free account or sign in. Your analyses and chat history are saved privately to your account — no one else can see your data.

---

### Step 2 — Fill in Your Company Profile

<!-- 🖼️ ADD SCREENSHOT: Company Profile form -->
> `[ Screenshot: Profile form with fields filled in ]` 

Enter your company details:

| Field | Example | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Company Name** | Acme Oy | Shown in your report |
| **Sector** | Healthcare / Finance / Tech… | Determines which regulations apply |
| **Employees** | 300 | CSRD threshold (250+), NIS2 scoping |
| **Country** | Finland | EU jurisdiction check |
| **Annual Revenue (€)** | 5,000,000 | DORA / CSRD thresholds |
| **Processes personal data** | ✅ | Triggers GDPR analysis |
| **Deploys AI systems** | ✅ | Triggers EU AI Act analysis |
| **Financial entity** | ☐ | Triggers DORA analysis |
| **Critical infrastructure** | ☐ | Triggers NIS2 analysis |
| **Listed company** | ☐ | Triggers CSRD analysis |
| **Current practices** | "ISO 27001 certified, DPO appointed" | The AI compares these against obligations |

Click **▶ Run Analysis** when ready.

### Step 4 — Review Your Dashboard

---

### Step 5 — Explore Your Compliance Gaps

<!-- 🖼️ ADD SCREENSHOT: Gaps tab with filters -->
> `[ Screenshot: Gaps tab with regulation filter and expanded gap card ]`

The **Gaps** tab lists every identified compliance gap:

- 🔴 **Critical (8.5–10)** — Severe penalties, zero current practice
- 🟠 **High (7–8.5)** — Major legal exposure
- 🟡 **Medium (5–7)** — Partial coverage, gaps remain
- 🟢 **Low (2.5–5)** — Minor gaps

Each gap shows:
- The exact regulation article (e.g. *GDPR Article 35 — Data Protection Impact Assessment*)
- What your company currently does (or doesn't do)
- The specific gap identified
- Cross-regulation dependencies (e.g. *"AI Act Article 9 also requires this"*)
- Risk score with justification

**Filter** by regulation, **sort** by risk or article, or **export to CSV**.

---

### Step 6 — Act on Your Action Plan

<!-- 🖼️ ADD SCREENSHOT: Action plan tab -->
> `[ Screenshot: Action plan with checkboxes and effort estimates ]`

The **Actions** tab turns your gaps into a concrete roadmap:

| Priority | What it means | Effort |
|:---:|---|:---:|
| 🔴 **Critical** | Immediate legal risk — act now | 4–8 weeks |
| 🟠 **High** | Significant exposure — address this quarter | 2–4 weeks |
| 🟡 **Medium** | Important gaps — schedule soon | 1–2 weeks |
| 🟢 **Low** | Minor gaps — next cycle | 2–5 days |

✅ Check off actions as you complete them — progress is saved automatically.

---

### Step 7 — Chat with the AI About Any Gap

<!-- 🖼️ ADD SCREENSHOT: Chat panel open on an action -->

The AI has full context of the regulation text, your company profile, and the specific gap — giving you grounded, citation-backed answers.

**General chat** is also available for broader compliance questions.

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific in "Current Practices"** — the more detail you give (e.g. *"Cookiebot CMP deployed, annual DPIA for HR system, ISO 27001 certified since 2022"*), the more accurate the gap analysis.
- **Use multiple regulations** — check all that apply. GDPR + AI Act together surfaces compound obligations the AI Cross-Reference Graph discovers automatically.
- **Start with Critical gaps** — filter to priority 1 first, tackle those before moving to lower priorities.
- **Ask the AI** — for any gap you don't understand, use the chat. It explains requirements in plain English with the actual article text as context.
